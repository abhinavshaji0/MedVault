Getting Started

Before using MedVault, you'll need to set up a few things. Follow these simple steps to get started with blockchain-based medical records.

What is MetaMask?

MetaMask is a cryptocurrency wallet and gateway to blockchain apps. It's available as a browser extension and mobile app, allowing you to interact with the Ethereum blockchain. For MedVault, MetaMask serves as your secure identity and allows you to control access to your medical records.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No technical knowledge is required to use MedVault. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. While blockchain technology powers the backend, the user experience is similar to any modern web application. We handle all the complex technical aspects so you can focus on managing your health records.

Is MedVault free to use?

MedVault offers a free basic plan that allows you to store and manage your medical records. There may be small transaction fees for certain blockchain operations, which are minimal. Premium features for healthcare providers and institutions are available with subscription plans.
